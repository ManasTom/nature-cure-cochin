Research shows that the regimen is highly effective in a host of lifestyle-related conditions that are unmet in mainstream treatments, and accordingly, patients are admitted for relief, prevention, and healing.

The patients who are being treated here are convinced that naturopathy is more than just a medical science; it is a way of life. They also learn to live in harmony with nature.
