Privacy Policy

At Cardinal Padiyara Naturecure Yogashram, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide us while using our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with the best possible service:

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details submitted through our website forms (e.g., appointment booking or enquiry forms).
  • Usage Data: Includes information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited on our website.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To schedule and manage appointments.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send updates about our services, special offers, or policy changes (if consented).
  • To improve our website and ensure a better user experience.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. This includes encryption, secure servers, and limited access to your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Your information may be shared with trusted third-party services (e.g., email providers) solely for fulfilling our services, provided they adhere to strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have about you.
  • Request corrections to your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, unless required for legal purposes.
